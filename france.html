Survival guide

Visa

After you get your acceptation letter, you might need to apply for a visa, the instructions for the application process can be found at the Campus France website. As Erasmus Mundus student you don't need to pay for the application.

1 Year or 6 Months Visa

If you get this type of visa, you have to pay a 50 eur stamp, that you can pay either online or at a Tabac shop.

3 Month Visa + Residence Permit

If you get a 3 months visa with the label "C. SEJ A SOLLIC" you need to apply for the residence permit as soon as posible. The process might be different than ours due COVID, but you should receive a mail from the International Relations Office with the detailed instructions, they might ask you to send your documents by post, but you can buy an envelope and leave it in their mailbox here

Registration at University

During your first days in Nancy you would receive a schedule for an appointment to the university registration office to be registered and getting a student card, remember to bring with you the following items so they can make copies and register you in (ORIGINALS ARE REQUIRED):

  • Birth certificate
  • Mark sheets
  • Diplomas
  • Academic transcripts

Transport

At the train station you can find the STAN office you can get a student transport pass (Fatima, I need yourt help for this one since I never got mine :P).
